Synopsis

Indian cuisine is a rich blend of the subtle use of various Spices, Herbs and Vegetables grow all over the country, which has been known for centuries as the Land of Spices. Its trade was the one of the most Important commercial activities that drove the world economy from the medieval times to the modern times. The regional cuisines of the country have been greatly influenced by an amazing variety of dishes, cooking techniques and a pervasive practice of vegetarianism among many communities, reflecting the great ethnic diversity of the subcontinent.
